# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

RaceWeek Strategist should be a useful, respectful project for contributors and users. We expect participants to:

- be kind and direct;
- assume good intent while addressing impact;
- keep disagreements focused on the work;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks;
- respect privacy, credentials, and sensitive user data.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers using the contact path in `SECURITY.md` if no dedicated community contact has been published yet.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other moderation steps when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
